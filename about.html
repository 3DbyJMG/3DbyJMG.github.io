About Us

Crafting Dreams in Three Dimensions


Founded by Julia M. Gersey, 3D by JMG aims to spread 3D printing knowledge, catering to hobbyists, makers, and aspiring young minds, inspired by Julia's journey from discovering 3D printing during her time at Carnegie Mellon University. With a commitment to accessibility, we leverage popular platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube to continuously share informative content. Julia and 3D by JMG are dedicated in their mission to combine affordable pricing, artistic expression, and educational initiatives, as she concludes her undergraduate journey and embarks on a Ph.D. program in Computer Science.
